Payscale

  • Caller: $125, plus potential attendance bonus
  • Musician: $125/each with a max of $500/band, plus potential attendance bonus
  • Sound: $75
  • Open band leader: $150
  • Open band anchor: same as standard musicians
  • Open band sound: if only one person is running sound for the evening, extra $25
  • Family dance caller / musician: $30, plus an extra $45 if they're not also doing the evening dance
  • Hall manager: $15/session ($30/evening)

We also pay an attendance-based bonus to each performer, which varies based on our financial situation. As of 2023-08-29, we pay $1 for every attendee over 120. You can see our typical attendance on our graphs page.

For more details on how we handle the money, see our Attendance and Financials Sheet which we fill out at each dance.
